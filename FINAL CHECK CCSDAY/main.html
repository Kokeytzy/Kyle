College Day Events

ACT COLLEGE DAY

Welcome to our annual College Day event! This exciting occasion brings together students, faculty, and community members to celebrate the vibrant spirit of our college. Throughout the day, attendees will have the opportunity to explore various academic programs, engage in interactive workshops, and connect with peers and mentors. With inspiring guest speakers, lively performances, and informative sessions, College Day is designed to showcase the diverse opportunities available within our institution. Join us as we foster a sense of community, ignite passions, and inspire future leaders!

COSPLAY

Cosplay is a vibrant and engaging performance art where participants, known as cosplayers, dress up as characters from various media, including anime, video games, movies, and comic books. This creative expression allows individuals to embody their favorite characters, showcasing their craftsmanship and passion for the source material.

COURSE

Dive into the dynamic world of technology with our Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) course. This program equips students with essential skills in software development, networking, and system management, preparing them for a successful career in the IT industry.
